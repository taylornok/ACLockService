Services

We've got you covered.

Residential

You can bring your locks to our shop to have them rekeyed at your convenience. Our charges are reasonable and in most cases we can tell you over the phone what to expect.

• Door re-keying

• Lock replacement

• Door frame reinforcement

• Keypad locks installed

• High Security Deadbolts installed

• New Sliding glass door locks

Commercial

Commercial locksmith services from trained professionals deployed to your business location. Quality service including lock re-keying, replacement, and repair, exit systems, security upgrades, and keys made all business lock and door services.

• Lock replacement and upgrades

• Lock and door repairs

• Push bars installed

• Exit bars installed

• Door plates Installed and more

Auto & Boats

We have the right high security key machine to make your keys and then program them into your car.

• Car opening

• Transponder keys made

• Older keys made

• Key copies made

• Key programming
