Services

We've got you covered.

Residential

You can bring your locks to our shop to have them rekeyed or we can send one of our mobile Alameda locksmiths to your location at your convenience. Our charges are reasonable and in most cases we can tell you over the phone what to expect.

• Door re-keying

• Lock replacement

•Door frame reinforcement

•Keypad locks installed

•High Security Deadbolts installed

•New Sliding glass door locks

Commercial

Alameda commercial locksmith services from trained professionals deployed to your business location. Quality service including lock re-keying, replacement, and repair, exit systems, security upgrades, and keys made all business lock and door services.

• Commercial door re-keying

• Lock replacement and upgrades

• Lock and door repairs

• Push bars installed

• Exit bars installed

• Door plates Installed and more

Auto & Boats

We have the right high security key machine to make your keys and then program them into your car.

• Car opening

• Transponder keys made

• Older keys made

• Key copies made

• Key programming

A.C. Lock Service Inc offers locksmith service in and around Alameda and Contra Costa Counties. Visit our locksmith service page for your location!


  • Albany Locksmith Services


    Albany may be only 5.5 square miles but we love being a Albany locksmith working providing great service to the Albany residents and business owners.

  • Berkeley Locksmith Services

    Berkeley is a city on the east shore of the San Francisco Bay in Northern California—in northern Alameda County. It is bordered on the south by the cities of Oakland and Emeryville.

  • Emeryville Locksmith Services

    Emeryville California is a small city in Alameda County It is located in a corridor between the cities of Berkeley and Oakland, extending to the shore of San Francisco Bay.

  • Oakland Locksmith Services

    Oakland is a major West Coast port city and the busiest port for San Francisco Bay and all of Northern California. It is the third largest city in the San Francisco Bay Area. This is one of the largest areas we cover as an Oakland locksmith.

  • Alameda Locksmith Services

    Alameda County is a county in the United States of America state of California.It occupies most of the East Bay region of the San Francisco Bay Area.

  • Montclair Locksmith Services

    Montclair is located in the Oakland Hills in a valley formed by the Hayward Fault and is part of our Montclair locksmith coverage area. Our Montclair mobile locksmith shops can come to your location to make keys, repair or install locks, open locked doors, remove broken keys and more.

  • Orinda Locksmith Services

    Orinda is a city in Contra Costa County, California. Orinda's many parks and trails make it a destination for many Bay Area hikers and naturalists. As an Orinda locksmith, our gold is to provide the best service to our customers and in a timely manner at an affordable reasonable price..

  • Piedmont Locksmith Services

    Piedmont is a small city in Alameda County, California. It is surrounded by the city of Oakland. Piedmont is almost entirely zoned for single-family dwelling residential use. We provide complete residential locksmith services.

  • El Cerrito Locksmith Services

    El Cerrito ("little hill") is a city in Contra Costa County, California. It forms part of the San Francisco Bay Area with over 23 thousand residents. We are proud to provide quality El Cerrito locksmith services to 6,000 homeowners and commercial clients.

  • Lafayette Locksmith Services

    Lafayette is a city in Contra Costa County, California, located between Berkeley and Walnut Creek. We provide quality residential and commercial locksmith services in timely manner.
